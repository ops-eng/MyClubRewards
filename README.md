# MyClubRewards

* NO ads, NO tracking, NO external logos, NO account sign-in, NO internet required *

* "MyClubRewards" is a Full Privacy digital wallet! *

** "MyClubRewards" transforms the way you carry your cards - no logos, no clutter, just pure simplicity and privacy, in a sleek, lightweight app.
** Snap the front and back of any card - loyalty, club, rewards, insurance, or ID - and watch it become a sleek digital tile that mirrors your real card’s color.
** Each tile is crafted automatically using smart color extraction and OCR, so your collection looks vibrant and organized without any manual editing.

Why MyClubRewards feels different
- Instant capture: Point, shoot, and save. The app detects edges, corrects perspective, and cleans glare automatically.
- Smart recognition: OCR identifies card type and key details like member numbers or policy IDs.
- Privacy first: Everything stays on your device. No cloud uploads, no external logos, no tracking.
- Color‑adaptive tiles: Each card inherits its own hue or lets you pick one - your digital wallet becomes a living mosaic.
- Barcode regeneration: Show a crisp, scannable barcode at checkout or when claiming rewards.
- Insurance‑ready: Store policy numbers, hotline contacts, and claim details in one secure place.
- Offline comfort: Works without internet - perfect for travel or low‑signal areas.
- Liquid Glass design: A modern, translucent interface that feels tactile and alive.
- Light or dark mode: Seamlessly adapts to your iPhone’s appearance settings.
- No distractions: No ads, no pop‑ups, just your cards - beautifully displayed.

Built for speed and trust:
- MyClubRewards uses Apple’s latest frameworks - SwiftUI, Vision, and CryptoKit - to deliver smooth performance and secure local storage.
- Your cards load instantly, thumbnails are optimized for memory efficiency, and sensitive data is encrypted with your device’s passcode protection.

Perfect for:
- Shoppers who juggle multiple loyalty programs
- Club members who want quick access to their IDs
- Travelers who need insurance details handy
- Anyone who values privacy and clean design

* Your cards, your colours, your space.
* MyClubRewards isn’t just a wallet - it’s a gallery of your everyday life, woven together with precision and care.
