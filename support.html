MyClubRewards Support

Email
For support, bug reports, or data deletion requests email: MyClubRewards Support

Include in your message: app version, device model, iOS version, a short description of the issue, and steps to reproduce. Attach a screenshot if helpful.

How to Report a Bug

Send an email to MyClubRewards Bug Report with:

  • Short summary of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Expected vs actual behavior
  • App version (Settings → About), device model, iOS version
  • Attach screenshots or a short screen recording

Feature Requests

We welcome suggestions. Email feature ideas to MyClubRewards Suggestions. Include a short use case and why it helps your workflow.

Privacy and Legal Requests

For privacy or data deletion requests, contact MyClubRewards Privacy. Include the device identifier shown in Settings → About if available.
